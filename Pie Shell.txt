Ingredients:
-2c flour
-1/2 tsp salt
-1/2c Wesson oil
-1/4c milk (1% or 2%)

Directions:
-Mix ingredients together in a large bowl
-Use hands to form two balls
-Roll out between two sheets of wax paper

Notes:
-If the crust is too dry add small amounts of water to mixture until desired consistency
-for coconut cream pie, use both balls of dough
