Ingredients/Tools needed:
-Milk (as much as needed)
-Wide and shallow pan
-Spoon or spatula
-Thermometer

Directions:
-Pour the milk into the pan
-Heat over medium heat, stirring frequently
-Heat until milk registers 180-185 degrees Fahrenheit
	-Small bubbled should be forming around the edges of the pan
-Remove from heat, and allow to cool to around 130 degrees before using it
